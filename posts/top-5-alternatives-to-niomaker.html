Top 5 Alternatives to NioMaker - Sunday, 12 October 2025

NioMaker is a popular tool for exporting leads from LinkedIn Sales Navigator. While its free tier and affordable PRO plan are attractive, some users may need alternatives that offer a wider range of features, better security, or a different pricing structure.

If you're looking for a new solution, you've come to the right place. In this guide, we'll break down the top 5 alternatives to NioMaker, comparing their features, pricing, and unique strengths to help you find the perfect tool for your lead generation and outreach strategy.

1. Bindago

  • Website: https://bindago.com/
  • What it is: A complete desktop application for LinkedIn automation that exports leads from LinkedIn and Sales Navigator to CSV and includes a full suite of outreach tools. It can handle bulk connection requests, mass InMail campaigns, and multi-step message sequences.
  • Pricing: $9.99/month (flat rate for all features)
  • Key Features:
    • Export Sales Navigator lead lists and LinkedIn search results to CSV
    • Send bulk connection requests with personalized notes
    • Create multi-step messaging sequences for automated follow-ups
    • Send mass InMails to Sales Navigator leads
    • Runs on your local machine for maximum security
    • 10-day free trial (no credit card required)
  • Platform: Desktop App (Windows/Mac/Linux)
  • Best For: Professionals and small businesses who want a secure, affordable, and all-in-one tool that combines lead exporting with powerful outreach automation.

Why Bindago is the #1 Alternative: Bindago offers the same core lead export functionality as NioMaker but at a lower price and with a full suite of outreach tools included. Its main advantage is that it's an all-in-one solution; you can export your leads and immediately use them in automated outreach campaigns within the same app. As a desktop app, your LinkedIn credentials stay on your machine, providing better security than browser extensions. At just $9.99/month, Bindago offers unbeatable value.

2. Evaboot

  • Website: https://evaboot.com/
  • What it is: A Chrome extension designed to extract leads from LinkedIn Sales Navigator, with features for data cleaning, filtering false positives, and email enrichment.
  • Pricing: Starts at €29/month for about 2,000 lead extractions. A free plan is available with about 150 credits/month.
  • Best For: Users who need to extract and enrich leads from Sales Navigator and prioritize data cleaning.

3. PhantomBuster

  • Website: https://phantombuster.com/
  • What it is: A flexible automation tool that uses "Phantoms" for a wide range of actions, including extracting leads from Sales Navigator. It can be configured for complex workflows.
  • Pricing: Paid plans start at $69/month, with a limited free trial.
  • Best For: Tech-savvy users who need a highly customizable tool for scraping data and building complex automation workflows.

4. Wiza

  • Website: https://wiza.co/
  • What it is: A tool that specializes in extracting lead lists from Sales Navigator, verifying email addresses, and exporting clean CSV files. It is highly focused on providing accurate contact information.
  • Pricing: Offers credit-based pricing with pay-as-you-go options.
  • Best For: Users focused on cold email prospecting who need verified email addresses with high deliverability.

5. Surfe

  • Website: https://surfe.com/
  • What it is: A tool that helps you export Sales Navigator lead lists directly into a CSV or your CRM (e.g., HubSpot, Salesforce). It also offers data enrichment features.
  • Pricing: Free trial available. Pricing plans vary, with some including CSV export and CRM sync.
  • Best For: Teams using major CRM platforms who want seamless integration and data enrichment.

Conclusion: Which NioMaker Alternative Is Right for You?

Choosing the right tool depends on your main goal:

  • For the Best Value and Security: Bindago is the clear winner. It provides a comprehensive set of outreach features in addition to lead exporting for just $9.99/month, all while keeping your account credentials safe on your computer.
  • For Data Cleaning and Enrichment: Evaboot and Wiza are strong competitors to NioMaker, offering similar email enrichment features with different pricing models.
  • For CRM Integration: Surfe is an excellent choice if you want to sync your LinkedIn leads and enriched data directly with your CRM.
  • For Maximum Flexibility: PhantomBuster offers a powerful, albeit more complex, solution for a wide range of automation tasks beyond just contact enrichment.

While NioMaker is a solid tool for exporting leads, these alternatives offer a variety of features and price points to suit different needs. For users who want to not only gather lead data but also act on it with powerful outreach campaigns, Bindago provides an unbeatable combination of affordability, security, and functionality.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Are desktop apps safer than browser extensions and cloud-based tools?
A: Generally, yes. Desktop applications like Bindago run on your computer, so your LinkedIn login credentials are not stored on a third-party server, reducing the risk of data breaches.

Q: Can I get banned for using these tools?
A: All automation tools carry some risk as they are against LinkedIn's Terms of Service. However, tools that simulate human behavior with smart delays and have built-in safety limits (like Bindago) significantly reduce this risk. Always start with low volumes and use the tools responsibly.

Q: Do I need Sales Navigator to use these tools?
A: For most of these tools, especially those focused on lead enrichment and export (like NioMaker, Evaboot, and Wiza), a Sales Navigator subscription is highly recommended to get the best results.
