10 Best Instagram Tools for Automated DM Responses in 2025 - Saturday, 15 November 2025

Automated replies on Instagram are a crucial tool for businesses aiming to provide immediate responses to frequent questions, boost customer happiness, and reclaim valuable time for other important tasks. Whether you are handling customer support, generating leads, or building a community, setting up automated DM replies guarantees that every message is acknowledged, even outside of business hours.

In this detailed overview, we will examine the 10 leading Instagram tools for automated DM responses in 2025, evaluating their functionalities, cost, and distinct features to assist you in selecting the ideal option for your company's requirements.

1. IGdm Pro - Best Overall for Instagram DM Automation

Website: https://igdm.me

IGdm Pro is a robust desktop application created specifically for automating and managing Instagram DMs. It is recognized as the most feature-rich and economical choice for companies concentrating on Instagram interaction.

Key Features

  • Automatic Message Replies: Configure automatic replies based on keywords to immediately answer incoming messages.
  • Automated Welcome Messages: Dispatch customized greetings to new followers shortly after they follow you.
  • Bulk DM Sending: Send large-scale messages to a maximum of 1,000 users with complete personalization.
  • Comment-to-DM Automation: Keep an eye on any Instagram post for comments and send DMs to commenters automatically.
  • Message Scheduling: Set up messages to be sent at particular times for increased engagement.
  • Conversation Management: Use labels and reminders to organize your inbox.

Pricing

  • Standard Plan: $8.99/month for up to 3 Instagram accounts.
  • Scale Plan: $19.99/month for unlimited Instagram accounts.
  • A 10-day free trial is offered.

2. ReplyRush - Best for Universal Triggers

Website: https://replyrush.com

ReplyRush is centered on Instagram-first automation, featuring strong universal triggers that can react to posts, Reels, Stories, and comments.

Pricing

  • Free Plan: $0/month (1 Instagram account, ~1,500 DMs/month)
  • Lite: $10/month
  • Boost: $25/month
  • Max: $100/month

3. ManyChat - Best Multi-Channel Platform

Website: https://manychat.com

ManyChat is a reputable multi-channel chatbot platform with solid Instagram DM automation features and an official partnership with Meta.

Pricing

  • Free Plan: Available for up to 1,000 contacts.
  • Pro Plan: Starting at $15/month (scales with the number of contacts).

4. InstantDM - Best Budget-Friendly Option

Website: https://instantdm.com

InstantDM provides Instagram-first DM automation with a substantial free tier and affordable pricing for its premium plans.

Pricing

  • Free Plan: Up to 1,500 automations/month.
  • Growth Plan: ~$6/month.
  • Legend Plan: ~$9/month with unlimited automations.

5. InstaChamp - Best for Q&A Automation

Website: https://app.mobilemonkey.com/instachamp

InstaChamp by MobileMonkey is designed for Q&A automation, offering keyword-based replies and story mention automation.

Pricing

  • Starting at $9.95/month for fundamental auto-reply features.

6. SendPulse - Best for Multi-Channel Marketing

Website: https://sendpulse.com

SendPulse is a broad marketing platform that offers Instagram chatbot and auto-reply functionalities in addition to email and SMS marketing.

Pricing

  • Pricing is dependent on the selected features and is part of a larger marketing suite.

7. AutoDMReply - Best for High Volume

Website: https://autodmreply.com

AutoDMReply is tailored for high-volume DM automation, providing templates for posts, Reels, and comments.

Pricing

  • Starter: $9/month (up to ~25,000 DMs/month).
  • Creator: $19/month (up to ~100,000 DMs/month).
  • Pro: $29/month (up to ~300,000 DMs/month).

8. ChatBoosty - Best for Enterprise

Website: https://chatboosty.com

ChatBoosty delivers enterprise-level DM automation with sophisticated personalization and scheduling options.

Pricing

  • Free trial is available.
  • Starter Plan: ~$150/month for 500 contacts.

9. GoSky AI - Best Flat-Rate Pricing

Website: https://goskyai.com

GoSky AI has straightforward, flat-rate pricing for essential Instagram auto-reply features.

Pricing

  • Starting at ~$12/month (flat rate).

10. SuperProfile - Best Community-Backed Option

Website: Various

SuperProfile and other community-supported tools provide basic auto-reply functions with less complexity.

Pricing

  • Reported to be around $29/month.

Conclusion

Instagram auto-reply automation is vital for modern businesses aiming to deliver immediate customer service and capture leads. While all the tools on this list have their merits, IGdm Pro is the top overall choice for businesses that are focused on Instagram automation.
