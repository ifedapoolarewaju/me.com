Top 5 Alternatives to NioLeads - Thursday, 27 November 2025

NioLeads is a well-known Chrome extension for exporting leads from LinkedIn Sales Navigator. While it's a handy tool, its pricing is not public, and some users may have security concerns about browser extensions or be looking for more comprehensive outreach features.

If you're looking for a different solution, you're in the right place. In this article, we'll explore the top 5 alternatives to NioLeads, comparing their features, pricing, and unique strengths to help you find the perfect fit for your lead generation and outreach strategy.

1. Bindago

  • Website: https://bindago.com/
  • What it does: A comprehensive desktop application for LinkedIn automation that not only exports LinkedIn and Sales Navigator leads to CSV but also includes a full suite of outreach tools. It handles bulk connection requests, mass InMail campaigns, and multi-step message sequences.
  • Pricing: $9.99/month (flat rate for all features)
  • Key Features:
    • Export Sales Navigator lead lists and LinkedIn search results to CSV
    • Bulk connection requests with personalized notes
    • Multi-step messaging sequences for automated follow-ups
    • Mass InMail sending to Sales Navigator leads
    • Runs locally on your computer for maximum security
    • 10-day free trial (no credit card required)
  • Platform: Desktop App (Windows/Mac/Linux)
  • Best For: Professionals and small businesses looking for a secure, affordable, and all-in-one alternative that combines lead exporting with powerful outreach automation.

Why Bindago is the #1 Alternative: Bindago offers the same core lead export functionality as NioLeads but at a fraction of the cost and with a full suite of outreach tools included. Its main advantage is that it's an all-in-one tool; you can export your leads and immediately use them in automated outreach campaigns within the same application. Furthermore, as a desktop app, your LinkedIn credentials remain on your machine, offering superior security compared to browser extensions. At just $9.99/month, Bindago provides unbeatable value.

2. Evaboot

  • Website: https://evaboot.com/
  • What it does: A Chrome extension specifically designed to extract leads from LinkedIn Sales Navigator with data cleaning, false positive filtering, and email enrichment.
  • Pricing: Starts at €29/month for ~2,000 lead extractions. A free plan is available with ~150 credits/month.
  • Best For: Users who need to extract and enrich leads from Sales Navigator and prioritize data cleaning.

3. Wiza

  • Website: https://wiza.co/
  • What it does: A tool that specializes in extracting lead lists from Sales Navigator, verifying email addresses, and exporting clean CSV files. It's highly focused on providing accurate contact information for prospecting.
  • Pricing: Offers credit-based pricing with pay-as-you-go options.
  • Best For: Users focused on cold email prospecting who need verified email addresses with high deliverability rates.

4. PhantomBuster

  • Website: https://phantombuster.com/
  • What it does: A powerful and flexible automation tool that offers "Phantoms" for a wide range of actions, including extracting leads from Sales Navigator searches and lists. It can be configured for complex workflows.
  • Pricing: Paid plans start around $69/month, with a limited free trial available.
  • Best For: Tech-savvy users who need a highly customizable tool for scraping data and building complex automation workflows across multiple platforms.

5. Surfe

  • Website: https://surfe.com/
  • What it does: A tool that helps you export Sales Navigator lead lists directly into CSV or your CRM (HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive). It also offers data enrichment features to find contact information.
  • Pricing: Free trial available. Pricing plans vary, with some including CSV export and CRM sync.
  • Best For: Teams using major CRM platforms who want seamless integration and data enrichment capabilities.

Conclusion: Which NioLeads Alternative is Right for You?

Choosing the right tool depends on your primary goal:

  • For the Best Value and Security: Bindago is the clear winner. It provides a comprehensive set of outreach features in addition to lead exporting for just $9.99/month, all while keeping your account credentials safe on your computer.
  • For Data Cleaning and Enrichment: Evaboot and Wiza are strong competitors to NioLeads, offering similar email enrichment features with different pricing models.
  • For CRM Integration: Surfe is an excellent choice if you want to sync your LinkedIn leads and enriched data directly with your CRM.
  • For Maximum Flexibility: PhantomBuster offers a powerful, albeit more complex, solution for a wide range of automation tasks beyond just contact enrichment.

While NioLeads is a solid tool for exporting leads, these alternatives offer a variety of features and price points to suit different needs. For users who want to not only gather lead data but also act on it with powerful outreach campaigns, Bindago provides an unbeatable combination of affordability, security, and functionality.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Are desktop apps safer than browser extensions and cloud-based tools?
A: Generally, yes. Desktop applications like Bindago run on your computer, so your LinkedIn login credentials are not stored on a third-party server, reducing the risk of data breaches.

Q: Can I get banned for using these tools?
A: All automation tools carry some risk as they are against LinkedIn's Terms of Service. However, tools that simulate human behavior with smart delays and have built-in safety limits (like Bindago) significantly reduce this risk. Always start with low volumes and use the tools responsibly.

Q: Do I need Sales Navigator to use these tools?
A: For most of these tools, especially those focused on lead enrichment and export (like NioLeads, Evaboot, and Wiza), a Sales Navigator subscription is highly recommended to get the best results.
