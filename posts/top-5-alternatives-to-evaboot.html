Top 5 Alternatives to Evaboot - Tuesday, 11 November 2025

Evaboot is a widely-used Chrome extension for pulling lead data from LinkedIn Sales Navigator, tidying up the information, and sourcing professional email addresses. While it is a robust tool, its subscription starts at €29/month, prompting some users to seek more budget-friendly alternatives, options with enhanced security, or a wider array of outreach functionalities.

If you are in the market for a different tool, you have come to the right place. This article will highlight the top 5 substitutes for Evaboot, breaking down their features, cost, and individual advantages to help you select the ideal solution for your lead generation and outreach efforts.

1. Bindago

  • Website: https://bindago.com/
  • What it does: A full-featured desktop application for LinkedIn automation that not only exports leads to CSV but also comes with a complete set of outreach tools. It can manage bulk connection requests, large-scale InMail campaigns, and automated multi-step message sequences.
  • Pricing: $9.99/month (flat rate for all features)
  • Key Features:
    • Export Sales Navigator lead lists and LinkedIn search results to CSV
    • Handle bulk connection requests with personalized notes
    • Set up multi-step messaging sequences for automated follow-ups
    • Send mass InMail to Sales Navigator leads
    • Operates locally on your computer for maximum security
    • 10-day free trial (no credit card needed)
  • Platform: Desktop App (Windows/Mac/Linux)
  • Best For: Professionals and small businesses in need of a secure, cost-effective, all-in-one alternative that merges lead exporting with advanced outreach automation.

Why Bindago is the #1 Alternative: Bindago provides the same essential lead export capabilities as Evaboot but at a lower price point and with a comprehensive suite of outreach tools. Its primary benefit is being an all-in-one solution; you can export leads and immediately use them in automated outreach campaigns within the same app. As a desktop application, it also offers superior security by keeping your LinkedIn credentials on your local machine. At just $9.99/month, Bindago delivers exceptional value.

2. PhantomBuster

  • Website: https://phantombuster.com/
  • What it does: A highly adaptable automation tool that provides "Phantoms" for a broad spectrum of tasks, including pulling leads from Sales Navigator searches and lists. It can be set up for intricate workflows.
  • Pricing: Paid plans begin at approximately $69/month, with a limited free trial offered.
  • Best For: Technically-inclined users who require a highly flexible tool for data scraping and constructing complex automation workflows across various platforms.

3. Wiza

  • Website: https://wiza.co/
  • What it does: A specialized tool for extracting lead lists from Sales Navigator, confirming email addresses, and exporting organized CSV files. It is heavily focused on delivering precise contact information for prospecting.
  • Pricing: Utilizes a credit-based model with pay-as-you-go options.
  • Best For: Users concentrating on cold email prospecting who require verified email addresses with high deliverability.

4. Surfe

  • Website: https://surfe.com/
  • What it does: A tool that facilitates exporting Sales Navigator lead lists directly to CSV or your CRM (such as HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive). It also includes data enrichment features to locate contact information.
  • Pricing: Free trial is available. Pricing plans differ, with some offering CSV export and CRM synchronization.
  • Best For: Teams that use major CRM platforms and desire smooth integration and data enrichment features.

5. SalesXporter

  • Website: https://salesxporter.com/
  • What it does: A Chrome extension created for exporting leads from LinkedIn Sales Navigator to CSV or Excel, with an option for email enrichment.
  • Pricing:
    • ~$27/month for 2,000 leads/month
    • ~$47/month for 5,000 leads/month
  • Best For: Users with consistent monthly export needs who want a simple, no-fuss extension for exporting leads.

Conclusion: Which Evaboot Alternative Is Right for You?

Selecting the best tool hinges on your main objective:

  • For the Best Value and Security: Bindago is the standout choice. It offers a full suite of outreach features in addition to lead exporting for only $9.99/month, all while ensuring your account credentials remain safe on your computer.
  • For Data Cleaning and Enrichment: Wiza and SalesXporter are strong rivals to Evaboot, providing similar email enrichment features with different pricing structures.
  • For CRM Integration: Surfe is a great option if you need to sync your LinkedIn leads and enriched data directly with your CRM.
  • For Maximum Flexibility: PhantomBuster presents a powerful, though more intricate, solution for a broad range of automation tasks beyond just contact enrichment.

While Evaboot is a reliable tool for data enrichment, these alternatives provide a range of features and price points to meet various needs. For users who wish to not only collect lead data but also act on it with effective outreach campaigns, Bindago offers an unparalleled mix of affordability, security, and functionality.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Are desktop apps safer than browser extensions and cloud-based tools?
A: Generally, yes. Desktop applications like Bindago run on your computer, so your LinkedIn login credentials are not stored on a third-party server, reducing the risk of data breaches.

Q: Can I get banned for using these tools?
A: All automation tools carry some risk as they are against LinkedIn's Terms of Service. However, tools that simulate human behavior with smart delays and have built-in safety limits (like Bindago) significantly reduce this risk. Always start with low volumes and use the tools responsibly.

Q: Do I need Sales Navigator to use these tools?
A: For most of these tools, especially those focused on lead enrichment and export (like Evaboot, Wiza, and Surfe), a Sales Navigator subscription is highly recommended to get the best results.
