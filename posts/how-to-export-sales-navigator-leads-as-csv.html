How To Export Sales Navigator Leads As CSV - Tuesday, 07 September 2025

LinkedIn Sales Navigator stands as the definitive premium solution for B2B prospecting and professional lead discovery. Through its comprehensive search capabilities and advanced filtering mechanisms, this platform enables precise identification of high-value prospects aligned with your business objectives.

The platform delivers sophisticated targeting options encompassing organizational parameters such as company scale, sector classification, executive hierarchy, and dynamic indicators like organizational transitions or personnel movements. These Sales Navigator search capabilities reveal qualified candidates that precisely match your target customer demographics.

Extracting these Sales Navigator prospects as CSV becomes essential for numerous operational requirements. Common scenarios include integrating prospect data into customer relationship management platforms such as Salesforce, HubSpot, or Pipedrive to enhance pipeline orchestration and lead nurturing processes.

This comprehensive guide demonstrates the complete process of extracting prospect information from LinkedIn Sales Navigator search results into CSV/Excel format utilizing the Bindago automation platform.

Understanding the Bindago Platform

Bindago represents a specialized automation solution engineered specifically for LinkedIn and Sales Navigator workflows. While CSV extraction constitutes a core functionality, the platform extends beyond data export to encompass bulk connection request automation, personalized InMail campaign management, and sophisticated message sequence orchestration for comprehensive Sales Navigator prospect engagement.

Initiating Your Sales Navigator Search Process

The foundation of effective prospect export begins with strategic search execution within LinkedIn Sales Navigator. Leverage the platform's advanced filtering capabilities to identify prospects matching your ideal customer parameters.

For demonstration purposes, this tutorial focuses on "VP Marketing" professionals within the technology sector, filtered by United States geographic location. Ensure proper navigation to the "Leads" section to access individual prospect profiles rather than organizational results (refer to the screenshot below).



Bindago Application Installation

Begin your setup process by accessing the official Bindago website at https://bindago.com. Select the appropriate installation package corresponding to your operating system requirements. Upon completion of the download process, execute the installation file and follow the guided setup procedures to establish the application on your workstation.

LinkedIn Account Authentication Process

Establishing secure connectivity between Bindago and your LinkedIn account follows these systematic steps:

  • Launch the Bindago application from your desktop
  • Select "Click to login with Browser" to initiate the authentication sequence
  • Complete LinkedIn credential verification through the Chromium, Edge, or Firefox browser window
  • Verify Sales Navigator access permissions are activated on your LinkedIn account
  • Upon successful authentication, the browser automatically closes and returns control to Bindago



Sales Navigator URL Acquisition

Navigate back to your previously configured Sales Navigator search results interface. Access the browser's address field and copy the complete URL string from the search results page. The URL structure should resemble the following pattern: "https://www.linkedin.com/sales/search/people?query=..."

URL Integration Within Bindago

  • Within the Bindago application, navigate to the search functionality via the primary "search" menu option
  • Insert the copied Sales Navigator URL into the designated input field within the interface
  • This action synchronizes the qualified prospects from your Sales Navigator search results with the Bindago platform



Executing CSV Export Process

With your Sales Navigator prospect data successfully loaded within the Bindago interface, initiate the export procedure:

  • Locate and select the "Export as CSV" function positioned at the bottom of the application interface
  • The export process initiates immediately upon selection
  • Processing duration typically ranges from 2-3 minutes, varying based on prospect volume
  • Upon completion, a system prompt enables custom file naming and destination selection



The CSV output encompasses essential Sales Navigator prospect information including professional positioning, organizational details, geographic data, and comprehensive profile insights. Current export limitations support up to 1,000 Sales Navigator prospects per export operation to ensure optimal processing performance.

Exported Data Structure Analysis

The comprehensive CSV export from Sales Navigator encompasses critical prospect intelligence including:

  • Complete name and professional designation
  • Current organizational affiliation and role
  • Geographic positioning
  • LinkedIn profile reference URL
  • Professional background summary (availability dependent)

This structured data framework facilitates seamless integration with any customer relationship management system or lead management infrastructure, enabling streamlined prospect qualification workflows and comprehensive outreach campaign development. The standardized format ensures compatibility across diverse business development platforms while maintaining data integrity throughout the import process.

Whether your objective involves CRM population, lead scoring system integration, or comprehensive prospect database development, this automated export methodology transforms Sales Navigator prospect discovery into actionable business intelligence ready for immediate operational deployment.
