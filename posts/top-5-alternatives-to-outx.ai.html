Top 5 Alternatives to OutX.ai - Wednesday, 19 November 2025

OutX.ai is a well-regarded tool for scraping Sales Navigator and finding emails, but its $99/month price tag and cloud-based model have users searching for alternatives. Whether you need a more budget-friendly option, better security, or a wider range of features, there are excellent competitors available.

This guide explores the five best alternatives to OutX.ai, breaking down their pricing, features, and what makes them stand out.

1. Bindago

  • Website: https://bindago.com/
  • Overview: A comprehensive desktop suite for LinkedIn automation that not only exports leads but also includes a full range of outreach tools. It manages everything from bulk connection requests to mass InMail campaigns and automated message sequences.
  • Price: $9.99/month (flat rate)
  • Key Features:
    • Export lead lists from Sales Navigator and LinkedIn searches to CSV.
    • Send bulk connection requests with personalized notes.
    • Automate follow-ups with multi-step messaging sequences.
    • Dispatch mass InMail campaigns to Sales Navigator leads.
    • Operates locally on your machine for superior security.
    • Offers a 10-day free trial with no credit card needed.
  • Platform: Desktop App (Windows/Mac/Linux)
  • Best For: Professionals and small businesses seeking a secure, affordable, all-in-one tool that combines lead exporting with powerful outreach automation.

Why It’s the #1 Alternative: Bindago delivers the same lead export functionality as OutX.ai but at a lower cost and with a full suite of outreach tools. Its primary advantage is its all-in-one nature; you can export leads and immediately use them in automated campaigns within the same app. As a desktop tool, it also offers better security. For just $9.99/month, Bindago offers unmatched value.

2. Evaboot

  • Website: https://evaboot.com/
  • Overview: A Chrome extension built to extract leads from LinkedIn Sales Navigator, featuring data cleaning, false positive filters, and email enrichment.
  • Price: Starts at €29/month for approximately 2,000 lead extractions. A free plan offers about 150 credits/month.
  • Best For: Users who need to extract and enrich leads from Sales Navigator with a focus on data cleaning.

3. PhantomBuster

  • Website: https://phantombuster.com/
  • Overview: A versatile automation tool that uses "Phantoms" for various actions, including extracting leads from Sales Navigator. It can be configured for highly complex workflows.
  • Price: Paid plans begin at around $69/month, with a limited free trial.
  • Best For: Advanced users who require a highly customizable tool for data scraping and building intricate automation workflows.

4. Wiza

  • Website: https://wiza.co/
  • Overview: Specializes in extracting lead lists from Sales Navigator, verifying email addresses, and exporting clean data. It is highly focused on providing accurate contact info for prospecting.
  • Price: Credit-based with pay-as-you-go options.
  • Best For: Users focused on cold email outreach who need verified email addresses with high deliverability.

5. SalesXporter

  • Website: https://salesxporter.com/
  • What it does: A Chrome extension designed to export leads from LinkedIn Sales Navigator into CSV or Excel, with optional email enrichment.
  • Pricing:
    • ~$27/month for 2,000 leads/month
    • ~$47/month for 5,000 leads/month
  • Best For: Users with predictable monthly export volumes who need a straightforward, no-frills extension for exporting leads.

Conclusion: Which Alternative is Right for You?

Your choice of tool should align with your main objective:

  • For Unbeatable Value and Security: Bindago is the clear leader. It offers a comprehensive suite of outreach features alongside lead exporting for only $9.99/month, all while keeping your account credentials secure on your local machine.
  • For Data Enrichment: Evaboot, Wiza, and SalesXporter are strong contenders, providing email enrichment with different pricing models.
  • For Maximum Flexibility: PhantomBuster provides a powerful, though more complex, solution for a wide array of automation tasks.

While OutX.ai is effective for data enrichment, these alternatives offer a broader range of features and price points. For users who want to not only collect lead data but also act on it with robust outreach campaigns, Bindago provides an unparalleled mix of affordability, security, and functionality.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Are desktop applications safer than cloud-based tools?
A: Generally, yes. Desktop apps like Bindago run on your computer, meaning your LinkedIn credentials are not stored on a third-party server, which minimizes the risk of data breaches.

Q: Is there a risk of being banned for using these tools?
A: All automation tools carry some risk, as they are against LinkedIn's Terms of Service. However, tools that simulate human behavior with smart delays and safety limits (like Bindago) significantly reduce this risk. Always start with low volumes and use the tools responsibly.

Q: Do I need a Sales Navigator subscription to use these tools?
A: For most of these tools, particularly those focused on lead export and enrichment like OutX.ai, Evaboot, and Wiza, a Sales Navigator subscription is highly recommended to achieve the best results.
