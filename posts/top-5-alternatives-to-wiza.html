Top 5 Alternatives to Wiza - Monday, 1 December 2025

Wiza is a widely-used tool for extracting leads from LinkedIn Sales Navigator and enriching them with verified contact information. While it excels at this, its credit-based pricing and focused feature set may lead some users to look for alternatives with different pricing, broader capabilities, or enhanced security.

If you're exploring other options, this guide will walk you through the five best alternatives to Wiza, helping you find the right fit for your lead generation and outreach needs.

1. Bindago

Website: https://bindago.com/

Bindago is a desktop application that offers a full suite of LinkedIn automation tools. It not only exports leads from LinkedIn and Sales Navigator but also provides features for outreach, such as bulk connection requests, mass InMail campaigns, and automated message sequences.

Pricing: $9.99/month for all features.

Key Features:
  • Export Sales Navigator and LinkedIn search results to CSV
  • Automate connection requests with personalized notes
  • Create multi-step messaging sequences
  • Send mass InMails to Sales Navigator leads
  • Runs locally for improved security
  • 10-day free trial available

Why it's a great alternative: Bindago provides a comprehensive set of tools for both lead gathering and outreach at a very low price. Its desktop-based nature means your LinkedIn credentials are not stored on a third-party server, offering a security advantage.

2. Evaboot

Website: https://evaboot.com/

Evaboot is a Chrome extension designed to extract, clean, and enrich leads from Sales Navigator. It focuses on data quality by filtering out false positives.

Pricing: Starts at €29/month.

Best for: Users who prioritize high-quality, clean data from Sales Navigator.

3. OutX.ai

Website: https://outx.ai/

OutX.ai is a Sales Navigator scraper that also finds and verifies email addresses. It's a good option for users who need reliable contact information for their leads.

Pricing: $99/month for certain credit tiers.

Best for: Users who need verified email addresses and are willing to pay for them.

4. PhantomBuster

Website: https://phantombuster.com/

PhantomBuster is a powerful and flexible automation tool that can perform a wide range of actions, including extracting leads from Sales Navigator. It's highly configurable and can be used to build complex workflows.

Pricing: Paid plans start at $69/month.

Best for: Tech-savvy users who need a customizable tool for various automation tasks.

5. Surfe

Website: https://surfe.com/

Surfe helps you export Sales Navigator leads to a CSV or directly to your CRM (HubSpot, Salesforce, etc.). It also offers data enrichment to find contact information.

Pricing: Varies, with a free trial available.

Best for: Teams that use a major CRM and want seamless integration.

Conclusion

Choosing the right tool depends on your primary goal. For the best value and security, Bindago is a strong choice, offering a wide range of features for a low monthly price. If you need data cleaning and enrichment, Evaboot and OutX.ai are good options. For CRM integration, consider Surfe, and for maximum flexibility, PhantomBuster is a powerful, if more complex, solution.

While Wiza is a solid tool, these alternatives provide a variety of features and price points to suit different needs. For users who want to not only gather leads but also act on them with outreach campaigns, Bindago offers an unbeatable combination of affordability, security, and functionality.
