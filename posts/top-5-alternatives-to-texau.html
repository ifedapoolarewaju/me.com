Exploring the Best TexAu Alternatives in 2025


TexAu is a recognized leader in the automation space, enabling users to connect various web services to streamline workflows, including LinkedIn outreach. However, its usage-based pricing and cloud-based infrastructure lead many to seek alternatives that offer more predictable costs, enhanced security, or a more specialized feature set for LinkedIn.

If you are evaluating other options, this guide will walk you through the top 5 alternatives to TexAu, breaking down their features, pricing, and unique strengths to help you find the perfect tool for your LinkedIn strategy.

1. Bindago

  • Website: https://bindago.com/
  • What it is: A comprehensive desktop application for LinkedIn automation that manages everything from bulk connection requests and mass InMail campaigns to automated message sequences and CSV exports. Because it runs locally, your credentials are never shared with a third-party server.
  • Price: $9.99/month (flat rate for all features)
  • Core Features:
    • Bulk connection requests with personalized notes
    • Multi-step messaging sequences
    • Mass InMail sending to Sales Navigator leads
    • Bulk withdrawal of pending invitations
    • Export LinkedIn search results to CSV
    • Runs locally for maximum security
    • 10-day free trial (no credit card required)
  • Platform: Desktop App (Windows/Mac/Linux)
  • Ideal For: Professionals and small businesses looking for a secure, affordable, and all-in-one alternative to TexAu for LinkedIn-specific automation.

Why Bindago is the #1 Alternative: Bindago offers the core LinkedIn automation features of TexAu but at a fraction of the cost and with a simpler, more focused approach. Its main advantage is security; as a desktop application, your LinkedIn credentials remain on your machine, which is a critical factor for users wary of cloud-based platforms. At just $9.99/month, Bindago offers unbeatable value for a complete and secure automation toolkit.

2. Expandi.io

  • Website: https://expandi.io/
  • What it is: A powerful cloud-based automation tool that mimics human behavior with smart sequences, connection requests, and follow-ups. It also offers multi-channel capabilities (LinkedIn + Email).
  • Price: Starts at US$99/month per user/seat.
  • Ideal For: Agencies and teams needing a premium cloud-based solution with advanced safety features and multi-channel outreach.

3. PhantomBuster

  • Website: https://phantombuster.com/
  • What it is: A flexible automation platform that offers "Phantoms" for a wide range of actions, including extracting leads from LinkedIn and running outreach campaigns. It's highly customizable and can be integrated into complex workflows.
  • Price: Paid plans start from about $30-$50/month depending on usage.
  • Ideal For: Tech-savvy users who need a highly customizable tool for scraping data and building complex automation workflows across multiple platforms.

4. Dripify

  • Website: https://dripify.com/
  • What it is: A specialized cloud-based tool for creating LinkedIn drip campaigns with advanced sequence logic, automated follow-ups, and detailed analytics dashboards.
  • Price: Starts at US$39/month for the basic plan.
  • Ideal For: Users who want to focus on sophisticated drip campaigns and are willing to pay for advanced analytics and lead management features.

5. Snov.io

  • Website: https://snov.io/
  • What it is: A platform that supports managing and automating various outreach tasks, including LinkedIn actions. It's known for its email-finding capabilities and multi-channel campaigns.
  • Price: Basic plans start from ~US$39-49/month based on usage.
  • Ideal For: Users who need a combination of email and LinkedIn outreach, with a strong focus on email verification and sourcing.

Final Recommendation: Which TexAu Alternative Is Best?

The right tool for you depends on your specific needs:

  • For the Best Combination of Value and Security: Bindago is the top choice. It offers a full suite of features for just $9.99/month while ensuring your account credentials remain secure on your computer.
  • For Advanced Cloud-Based Features: Expandi.io is a powerful, though more expensive, option for those who need cloud-based multi-channel outreach and AI capabilities.
  • For Maximum Flexibility: PhantomBuster provides a highly customizable solution for users who need to build complex workflows across different platforms.
  • For a Focus on Drip Campaigns: Dripify is a strong choice if you want a tool specifically designed for creating and analyzing sophisticated LinkedIn drip campaigns.
  • For Email-Centric Outreach: Snov.io is a great option if your outreach strategy is heavily reliant on email in addition to LinkedIn.

While TexAu is a versatile tool, these alternatives show that you can achieve powerful LinkedIn automation with different features and at various price points. For most users focused on LinkedIn, Bindago offers the ideal mix of features, security, and affordability.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Are desktop apps more secure than cloud-based tools?
A: Yes, generally. Desktop applications like Bindago run on your own machine, meaning your LinkedIn login details are not stored on a third-party server, which reduces the risk of data breaches.

Q: Can using these tools lead to an account ban?
A: All automation tools carry some level of risk as they are against LinkedIn's Terms of Service. However, tools that mimic human behavior with smart delays and have built-in safety limits, like Bindago, significantly lower this risk. It's always best to start with low activity volumes and use the tools responsibly.

Q: Is Sales Navigator required to use these tools?
A: While not strictly necessary, it is highly recommended. Sales Navigator offers superior search filters and higher commercial use limits, which helps you get the most out of any automation tool.
