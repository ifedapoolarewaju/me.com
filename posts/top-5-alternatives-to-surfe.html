Top 5 Surfe Competitors - Friday, 21 November 2025

Surfe is a widely-used tool for exporting Sales Navigator leads to CSVs and CRMs like HubSpot, Salesforce, and Pipedrive. While it excels at data enrichment and CRM integration, its cost can be a factor, and many users seek alternatives that are more budget-friendly, offer stronger security, or provide a wider array of outreach capabilities.

If you are looking for a different solution, you are in the right place. This guide will explore the top five alternatives to Surfe, comparing their features, pricing, and unique advantages to help you find the ideal fit for your lead generation and outreach efforts.

1. Bindago

  • Website: https://bindago.com/
  • What it does: A full-featured desktop application for LinkedIn automation that exports LinkedIn and Sales Navigator leads to CSV and includes a comprehensive suite of outreach tools. It manages bulk connection requests, mass InMail campaigns, and multi-step message sequences.
  • Pricing: $9.99/month (flat rate for all features)
  • Key Features:
    • Export Sales Navigator lead lists and LinkedIn search results to CSV
    • Send bulk connection requests with personalized notes
    • Create multi-step messaging sequences for automated follow-ups
    • Dispatch mass InMail campaigns to Sales Navigator leads
    • Runs locally on your computer for maximum security
    • Offers a 10-day free trial (no credit card required)
  • Platform: Desktop App (Windows/Mac/Linux)
  • Best For: Professionals and small businesses seeking a secure, affordable, all-in-one tool that combines lead exporting with powerful outreach automation.

Why Bindago is the #1 Alternative: Bindago provides the same core lead export functionality as Surfe but at a significantly lower cost and with a full suite of outreach tools. Its main advantage is its all-in-one nature; you can export leads and immediately use them in automated campaigns within the same application. As a desktop app, it also offers superior security by keeping your LinkedIn credentials on your machine. At just $9.99/month, Bindago offers exceptional value.

2. Evaboot

  • Website: https://evaboot.com/
  • What it does: A Chrome extension designed to extract leads from LinkedIn Sales Navigator, clean the data, filter out false positives, and enrich it with emails.
  • Pricing: Starts at €29/month for ~2,000 lead extractions. A free plan is available with ~150 credits/month.
  • Best For: Users who need to extract and enrich leads from Sales Navigator and prioritize data cleaning.

3. PhantomBuster

  • Website: https://phantombuster.com/
  • What it does: A powerful and flexible automation tool that offers "Phantoms" for a wide range of actions, including extracting leads from Sales Navigator. It can be configured for complex workflows.
  • Pricing: Paid plans start around $69/month, with a limited free trial available.
  • Best For: Tech-savvy users who need a highly customizable tool for scraping data and building complex automation workflows.

4. Wiza

  • Website: https://wiza.co/
  • What it does: A tool specializing in extracting lead lists from Sales Navigator, verifying email addresses, and exporting clean CSV files. It is highly focused on providing accurate contact information.
  • Pricing: Offers credit-based pricing with pay-as-you-go options.
  • Best For: Users focused on cold email prospecting who need verified email addresses with high deliverability rates.

5. SalesXporter

  • Website: https://salesxporter.com/
  • What it does: A Chrome extension designed to export leads from LinkedIn Sales Navigator into CSV or Excel, with optional email enrichment.
  • Pricing:
    • ~$27/month for 2,000 leads/month
    • ~$47/month for 5,000 leads/month
  • Best For: Users with predictable monthly export volumes who need a straightforward extension for exporting leads.

Conclusion: Which Surfe Alternative Is Right for You?

Choosing the right tool depends on your primary goal:

  • For the Best Value and Security: Bindago is the clear winner. It provides a comprehensive set of outreach features in addition to lead exporting for just $9.99/month, all while keeping your account credentials safe on your computer.
  • For Data Cleaning and Enrichment: Evaboot, Wiza, and SalesXporter are strong competitors to Surfe, offering similar email enrichment features with different pricing models.
  • For Maximum Flexibility: PhantomBuster offers a powerful, albeit more complex, solution for a wide range of automation tasks beyond just contact enrichment.

While Surfe is a solid tool for CRM integration, these alternatives offer a variety of features and price points to suit different needs. For users who want to not only gather lead data but also act on it with powerful outreach campaigns, Bindago provides an unbeatable combination of affordability, security, and functionality.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Are desktop apps safer than browser extensions and cloud-based tools?
A: Generally, yes. Desktop applications like Bindago run on your computer, so your LinkedIn login credentials are not stored on a third-party server, reducing the risk of data breaches.

Q: Can I get banned for using these tools?
A: All automation tools carry some risk as they are against LinkedIn's Terms of Service. However, tools that simulate human behavior with smart delays and have built-in safety limits (like Bindago) significantly reduce this risk. Always start with low volumes and use the tools responsibly.

Q: Do I need Sales Navigator to use these tools?
A: For most of these tools, especially those focused on lead enrichment and export (like Surfe, Evaboot, and Wiza), a Sales Navigator subscription is highly recommended to get the best results.
